What SurgeWatch will do

Address-Based Risk

Enter a home address and see location-specific flood exposure based on elevation, nearby waterways, alerts, and live conditions.

Custom Rain Readiness Score™

A coded risk engine combines rainfall, gauge trends, flood alerts, FEMA mapping, elevation, and prototype engineering factors into one simple score.

Smart Notifications

Users will be able to watch an address and receive alerts when local flood risk rises or dangerous conditions develop.

Early prototype in development.

SurgeWatch.live is being built as a civic engineering project focused on real-time flood awareness, public safety, infrastructure monitoring, and early-warning notifications.
