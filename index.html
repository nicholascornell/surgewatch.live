What SurgeWatch will do

Address-Based Risk

Enter a home address and see location-specific flood exposure based on elevation, nearby waterways, alerts, and live conditions.

Weighted Flood Risk Score

A risk engine combines rainfall, NWS alerts, FEMA data, gauge conditions, elevation, drainage, and tide influence into one transparent score.

Engineering Site Profile™

Future layers can add soil readiness, slope, geology, drainage, and foundation context for deeper site intelligence.

Early prototype in development.

SurgeWatch.live is being built as a civic engineering project focused on real-time flood awareness, site intelligence, infrastructure monitoring, and early-warning notifications.
